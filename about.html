About me

Hello! My name is Sarah VanTilburg.

I'm a front-end developer who has a strong passion for HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My background in UX/UI design allows me to bring a unique and creative perspective to each project I undertake.

Prior to my career in web development, I proudly served in the United States Air Force for 5 years. During my time in the military, I learned the significance of discipline, attention to detail, and teamwork. My technical proficiency and UX/UI knowledge enable me to take a holistic approach to projects, considering both aesthetics and user experience.

When I'm not working, I love spending my free time hiking in nature, acrylic pouring, and experimenting with new recipes.
