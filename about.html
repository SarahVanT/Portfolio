About me

Hello! My name is Sarah VanTilburg.

I'm a passionate Front-End Developer, embarking on a unique journey from the world of Bioenvironmental Engineering to the realm of digital experiences. With a foundation in engineering problem-solving and an eye for design, I'm dedicated to crafting web solutions that seamlessly blend creativity and functionality.

Transitioning from Air Force service, I've mastered the art of turning complex challenges into innovative solutions. Today, my knack for deciphering intricate incidents finds its home in coding, where I create user-friendly interfaces that resonate with diverse audiences.

Beyond coding, you'll find me hiking, experimenting in the kitchen, strategizing over board games, and exploring new artistic endeavors through acrylic pouring. Let's connect and collaborate to bring captivating web experiences to life!
