About me

Hello! My name is Sarah VanTilburg.

I'm a front-end developer who is passionate about crafting exceptional web experiences. With expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as a background in UX/UI design. I bring a unique blend of technical skills and creative vision to every project.

Before diving into the world of web development, I proudly served in the United States Air Force for 5 years. This experience instilled in me the values of discipline, attention to detail, and teamwork, which I now apply to my work as a developer. Taking a holistic approach, I prioritize aesthetics and seamless user experiences. Success means captivating visuals and effortless user guidance.

In my free time, hiking in nature inspires me, and acrylic pouring fuels my creativity. In the kitchen, I experiment with flavors, always seeking improvement.
