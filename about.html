About me

Welcome to my website! I'm Sarah VanTilburg, a front-end developer with a deep appreciation for HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as well as a background in UX/UI design.

Before embarking on my journey in web development, I served in the United States Air Force for 5 years. During my time in the military, I learned the importance of discipline, attention to detail, and teamwork. The combination of my technical proficiency and UX/UI knowledge allows me to approach projects holistically, considering both the aesthetics and the user experience. I believe that a successful website or application is one that not only looks visually appealing but also effortlessly guides users through their journey.

Beyond coding, hiking in nature inspires me, and acrylic pouring fuels my creativity. In the kitchen, I experiment with flavors, always seeking improvement.
